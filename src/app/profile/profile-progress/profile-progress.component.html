Erasmus +

A separate application is not required for the Erasmus+ grant.
IO will contact you by email asking you to fill in and submit particular documents.

Necessary Documents

You have to carry out the following activities and to hand in the following documents before you leave Germany:

  • LA incl. your signature as well as the lecturers’ – if needed – and the faculty coordinator’s signature (should be handed in at the IO ServicePoint as soon as possible as it needs to be signed by the host university before your semester abroad starts. Before submitting your LA to the Service Point please upload onto your Mobility Online account.
  • Grant Agreement (fill in, sign, upload onto your Mobility Online account, and hand in the original at the IO ServicePoint)
  • Participation in Intercultural Training Part l and ll
  • Accomplish first OLS Language Test in the host university’s teaching language (if required, you will receive an invitation from the IO)

learning agreement (LA)

Recognition of the subjects you take at a foreign university is one of the most important issues when it comes to preparing a study period abroad. THI uses the European Credit Transfer System (ECTS) for all courses - a standardised system which applies throughout Europe. Course grades gained in countries outside Europe are also converted into ECTS points. This makes it easy for THI students to gain recognition for the grades they have achieved abroad. Essentially, there is a very wide range of options when it comes to selecting subjects, depending on the language skills required for the country you will be studying in.
The most important instrument for subject recognition is the Learning Agreement (LA - see download section for templates). It is a contract drawn up between you, THI and the partner university. When fully completed and signed prior to your departure, the LA guarantees recognition of the grades achieved abroad when you return to THI. The procedure is as follows:

  • If you choose a subject which has not been recognised previously, the relevant THI lecturer has to confirm the equivalence of the subject by signing the LA. For this, you have to provide them with a subject description and the valid study and examination regulations of your degree course.
  • Every LA must be signed by the student, the departmental coordinator and finally by the institutional coordinator in the IO.
  • The LA must be signed by the departmental and institutional coordinators at the host university.

! Note: The LA is only valid with ALL the necessary signatures. Otherwise, the grades you achieved abroad will not be recognised.
! Note: There is a special LA form for partner universities in the Erasmus+ programme.

After you return

Recognition of grades

Once your study semester is complete, your partner university sends your grade sheet (TOR = Transcript of Records) to the THI International Office. You are informed as soon as it has been received. The IO makes a certified copy which you have to submit to the SCS (Service Center Academic Affairs) along with a certified copy of the signed LA so that the grades and subjects can be credited to your degree programme. If you still have the original LA, please bring this along, too. Further information can be found in Moodle
